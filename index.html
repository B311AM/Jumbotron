About Bella M.

Pool

Within the past year, I've picked up Pool as one of my hobbies. I started when my mom found out we have a pool hall in Henderson (where we live), and wanted to take me and teach me how to play. When my mom was staioned in Korea in the early 2000's they would go to local bars in the city closest to their base and play pool almost every night. Often times she would compete in tournaments, and was rarely defeated. Even though she doesn't play as often anymore, she still has alot of her old skill and uses it to teach me how to play.
